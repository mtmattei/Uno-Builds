## Temporary Solution

Until you provide actual product images, you can:

1. **Use online placeholder service**: The app will attempt to load from URLs first
2. **Create simple placeholders**: Use any image editor to create 360×480px images with #F5F5F5 background
3. **Use stock photos**: Download free fashion model photos from Unsplash or Pexels

## Image Specifications

- **Background color**: #F5F5F5 (light gray) or #ECECEC
- **Product images**: Show model in walking/action pose
- **Format**: PNG or JPG
- **Quality**: High resolution for clear display on all devices
